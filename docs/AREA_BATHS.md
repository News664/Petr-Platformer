# Area design — The Sunken Baths (Region 2)

Theme sheet in `docs/AREAS.md` (stone versus water; verdigris/ink/marble/
candle-gold; silt sirens + cistern crabs; boss: Mother Lye; abilities:
Soften II mid-region, Self-Petrification at the wing Sanctuary).

Entry: the Square's drowned stair. Gate: a **cracked grate** under the
water — dash it (Chisel Dash, from the Quarry) and the water roars away,
opening the descent. Regions unlock each other across the hub: the map's
promise kept.

## Siren rules (the region's core enemy)

- Song radius ~220: any **softened** NPC inside walks toward the song
  instead of Amethyst's light. Stone is deaf; pushed statues are safe.
- Killed by a Chisel Dash, or by a heavy stone (mass ≥ 10) dropped onto
  her — "the stone ends the song."
- Design grammar: sirens both *threaten* escorts and can be *used* — a
  deliberately-placed siren can pull a softened NPC somewhere Amethyst
  cannot lead (planned rooms).
